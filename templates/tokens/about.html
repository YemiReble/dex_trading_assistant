About DEX Trading Assistant

AI-powered cryptocurrency analysis and recommendations

How It Works

Data Collection

We fetch real-time data from Dexscreener API, including price, volume, liquidity, and market cap information.

AI Analysis

Our algorithm analyzes multiple metrics to calculate a comprehensive score for each token.

Recommendations

Based on the analysis, we provide Buy, Hold, or Avoid recommendations with risk management guidance.

Analysis Methodology

Scoring System (0-100 points)

Volume Analysis (30 points)

  • • >$1M volume: 30 points
  • • >$100K volume: 20 points
  • • >$10K volume: 10 points

Price Movement (25 points)

  • • 0-20% gain: 25 points
  • • Small loss (-5-0%): 15 points
  • • High volatility (>20%): 5 points

Liquidity (25 points)

  • • >$500K liquidity: 25 points
  • • >$100K liquidity: 15 points
  • • >$50K liquidity: 10 points

Market Cap (20 points)

  • • $1M-$100M: 20 points
  • • >$100M: 15 points
  • • >$100K: 10 points

Recommendation Logic

BUY (Score ≥70)

High-quality tokens with strong fundamentals and positive momentum.

HOLD (Score 40-69)

Decent tokens that may be worth monitoring or holding existing positions.

AVOID (Score <40)

Tokens with poor fundamentals or high risk factors.

Risk Management Features

Volatility Index

We calculate a volatility index based on price movements across different timeframes (1h, 6h, 24h) to help you understand the risk level of each token.

Position Sizing

Our system suggests position sizes based on the recommendation strength and risk profile, typically 2-5% of portfolio for individual positions.

Stop Loss Levels

For BUY recommendations, we calculate suggested stop-loss levels (typically 10% below entry) to help limit potential losses.

Real-time Updates

Token data is updated every 5 minutes automatically, ensuring you have the latest information for making informed decisions.

Data Sources
